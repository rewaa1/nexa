# Orbix Vision

## Mission

Orbix builds digital ecosystems.

We create:

- Web Applications
- SaaS Platforms
- CRM Systems
- Mobile Applications
- AI Solutions

The website itself must demonstrate our capabilities.

---

## Core Narrative

Everything revolves around:

Orbit
Gravity
Connection
Evolution

Technology is represented as celestial systems.

Every service, project, interaction, and animation should feel part of a living universe.

---

## Emotional Journey

Users should experience:

1. Curiosity
2. Wonder
3. Discovery
4. Confidence
5. Trust
6. Excitement

---

## Experience Goal

Visitors should feel like they are travelling through a digital solar system rather than browsing a company website.
